Sports of Vatican City

The Vatican City National Football Team

The only sports in Vatican City is their soccer team. The Vatican City national football team was founded in 1972, the current president Domenico Ruggerio, and head coach Gianfranco Guadagnoli.

FIFA Affiliation

Vatican city happens to be the only recognized sovereign states that is not part of FIFA.

The start of Football In Vatican city

Vatican footballing history began in January of 1521 when the first match of Calcio Fiorentino(A early for of football and rugby.) was played in the Vatican in the Cortile del Belvedere. The first Vatican league was created in 1973 and was first won by employees of L'Osservatore Romano, the newspaper of the Holy See.
